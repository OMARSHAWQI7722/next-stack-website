About Next Stack

Learning that doesn't stop at the classroom door.

Next Stack is a student-led technology initiative under the 3ZERO Club at Albukhary International University. We exist to close the gap between academic education and the fast-moving tech industry.

We do this through practical experience, collaborative projects, technical workshops, and meaningful community initiatives — built and run by students, for students.

Through innovation, teamwork, and hands-on experience, we prepare students to become confident developers, sharp problem solvers, and future technology leaders.

🎯 Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and the real job market by empowering students with practical skills, industry exposure, and real-world experience — while creating meaningful impact within our community.

🚀 Our Vision

To become one of the leading student technology communities — inspiring innovation, developing future-ready talent, and creating opportunity through technology and collaboration.

Scratch Programming Workshop
